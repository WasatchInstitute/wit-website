Informational Meetings

Attending WIT is a remarkably different experience. To help you see just how different and to make sure you're prepared for the switch, we hold in-person meetings. These events last about an hour and provide a firsthand opportunity to learn about WIT and our philosophy, see our space, meet some of our staff and board members, and see what WIT students did last year. Most importantly, attending this meeting will give you a change to get answers to the questions you have about WIT and verify that WIT would be a good match for you and your student.

Our next informational meetings are:

- Thu 6 Aug 2015 at 7:00 pm
- Tue 18 Aug 2015 at 6:00 pm

The informational meetings are held at WIT, which is located at 120 W Vine St #200 in Murray (see the map). We are west of Intermountain Medical Center and north of the Murray Central TRAX/Frontrunner stations.
