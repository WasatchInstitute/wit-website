WHAT ARE SPRINTS?

A sprint is a 2-4 week period where students learn about a chosen theme that incorporates all their classes and subjects. Sprints include many field trips, guest speakers, activities, and more. Students present what they have learned at the end of the sprint through sprint presentations and sprint deliverables.
